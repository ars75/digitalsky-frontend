My drone is 250 grams. I want to apply to become a and I’m

Answer: First you need to create an account here on DroneStack. Once you have registered, you have to apply to become a pilot through your dashboard. When you apply to become a pilot, you have to upload your training certificate and fill in details for security clearance. These details will go to verifying authority.

Once you have your pilot license, you can apply for “Acquisition of drone” in your dashboard. After aquisition, you can apply for a unique identification number (UIN) for your drone.
